About

Jagran Youth Parliament is a platform designed to immerse India’s brightest minds in the democratic process. Through structured debates and interactive simulations, JYP builds leadership, develops communication skills, and inspires civic engagement. It is more than a competition—it's a transformative journey into the core of governance.

Our Mission

To empower youth with the knowledge, confidence, and leadership skills to actively engage in India’s democratic process and become the voices of progressive change.

How Jagran Youth Parliament Works

Experience the structure and depth of real parliamentary simulations designed to shape impactful leaders.

Roleplay as Parliamentarians
  • Take roles of MPs & Ministers
  • Engage in real issue debates
  • Follow parliamentary rules
Debate & Collaborate
  • 6-minute paired debates
  • Topic-based argumentation
  • Judged on articulation & logic
Develop Skills
  • Critical thinking
  • Policy understanding
  • Teamwork & diplomacy

Program Flow

Registration → Debate Rounds → Final Simulation → Recognition

Eligibility

Who Can Apply

Open to Undergraduate and Postgraduate students across India.

Selection Format

Paired debates on pre-decided motions judged on articulation, logic and collaboration.

Winners

Top teams will proceed to final parliamentary simulations and recognition.

Program Process

  • Selection through debate competition
  • 6-minute paired debates
  • Topics pre-decided and given in advance
  • Debates judged on logic, delivery and teamwork
